# 社区行为准则 | Community Code of Conduct

## 我们的承诺 | Our Pledge

我们希望所有参与者都能在一个尊重、开放且专注于建设性协作的环境中交流，不因经验水平、身份背景或观点差异受到骚扰或排斥。

We are committed to a respectful, open, and constructive environment for everyone, regardless of experience, identity, background, or viewpoint.

## 期望行为 | Expected Behavior

- 友善、具体地讨论问题，并尊重不同意见。
- 对代码、数据、教学内容和反馈给出可验证的依据。
- 尊重第三方内容的版权、署名和使用边界。
- 在发现错误或风险时，帮助说明影响和可行的修复方式。

- Discuss issues kindly and specifically, and respect differing views.
- Support claims about code, data, teaching content, and feedback with verifiable evidence.
- Respect copyright, attribution, and usage boundaries for third-party material.
- When reporting a problem or risk, help explain its impact and a practical path to resolution.

## 不可接受的行为 | Unacceptable Behavior

- 人身攻击、骚扰、歧视、威胁或持续干扰讨论。
- 未经许可发布他人的私人信息。
- 冒充刘辉本人，或声称本项目的生成内容得到刘辉本人认可。
- 明知有误仍提交虚假来源、伪造反馈或误导性教学结论。

- Personal attacks, harassment, discrimination, threats, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing another person's private information without permission.
- Impersonating 刘辉 or claiming that generated content is personally endorsed by him.
- Knowingly submitting fabricated sources, false feedback, or misleading coaching conclusions.

## 执行与报告 | Enforcement and Reporting

维护者可以编辑或移除违反本准则的 Issue、评论、提交或其他贡献，并可限制重复违规者参与。安全问题请按照 [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) 私密报告；其他行为问题可通过仓库所有者的 GitHub 主页联系。

Maintainers may edit or remove issues, comments, commits, or other contributions that violate this code, and may restrict repeated offenders. Report security issues privately as described in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md); for other conduct concerns, contact the repository owner through their GitHub profile.
